About Us

We are TEC Collage to Growth your Education

TEC is a private professional and higher educational Collage, that has been providing a productive alternative to higher education since 2010.

Partnering with one of the top UK Universities, University of Gloucestershire, TEC Collage is committed to ensuring that every student has the opportunity to fulfil their full potential to make outstanding graduate-level contributions to their own and to the country as a whole. GUSTO has already produced more than 15,000 professionals and graduates and most of them are now serving at high managerial and technical positions in renowned local and international companies.

We envision TEC Collage, to be the leading private Collage in Myanmar where all staff and students can learn, grow, and reach their full potential, as well as making a positive impact to the society.

TEC delivers a unique learning experience for Myanmar citizens to study and earn postgraduate degrees, bachelor degrees, diplomas and certificates that are accredited by competitive international partners and respected globally while working in their home country. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional diploma programmes; for students and working professionals at various stages of life. Each program upholds the same high standard of learning and prepares students to compete on an international level.
