TEC College Research Programme

The TEC College Research Programme (TECRP) is offered to JC1 students in Junior Colleges (JCs) and Year 5 students in Integrated Programme (IP) Schools.

The TECRP offers a variety of research topics from TEC’s four Colleges – College of Engineering, College of Science, College of Humanities & Social Sciences, and College of Business – as well as the National Institute of Education. It seeks to offer students with a keen interest in and aptitude for research the opportunity to engage in the process of intellectual inquiry by undertaking projects in a real research environment under the supervision of TEC faculty, scientists and researchers.

Participants in the TECRP will undertake eight months of research activities (from April to December), either individually or in a pair, culminating in the submission of a Research Paper (in January the following year) and an Oral Presentation Assessment (in late February/early March).

TEC Research Programme Junior Researcher (TECRPjr)

The TEC Research Programme Junior Researcher (TECRPjr) is a sister programme to the TEC Research Programme (TECRP).

It is targeted at Secondary 3 students in selected Secondary Schools, and Year 3 and 4 students in Integrated Programme (IP) Schools, offering research topics from TEC Colleges as well as the National Institute of Education.

TECRPjr will require students from the same school to work in a pair and be supported by a teacher mentor who will work closely with the students and their Supervisor.

Similar to the TECRP, participants in TECRPjr will undertake eight months of research activities under the charge of an TEC faculty. The period of research will run from April to December. TECRPjr will culminate in the submission of the Research Paper (in January the following year) and the Oral Presentation Assessment (in late February/early March).
